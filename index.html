Safety Cautions ⚠️

Keep Away From Flames

Always keep gas containers away from open flames and heat sources.

Use in Well-Ventilated Areas

Only use gas appliances in well-ventilated spaces to prevent gas buildup.

Regular Inspection

Regularly check hoses and connections for wear or damage.

Leak Detection

If you smell gas, turn off the supply, extinguish flames, and ventilate the area immediately.

Proper Storage

Store gas cylinders upright in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

No DIY Repairs

Never attempt to repair gas equipment yourself - always use a qualified technician.
